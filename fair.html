Spring 2019 Career Fair

January 30th and 31st at the ARC from 1-6 PM

Spring 2019 Engineering Career Fair

Need that summer internship? Looking for that full-time job? Want that real-world experience in a Co-Op? Come to the Illinois Engineering Spring 2019 Career Fair on January 30th - 31st from 1pm-6pm at the ARC! Representatives from various companies will be on campus looking for candidates like YOU! This is an event you won't want to miss!

Interested in Volunteering?

As one of the largest career fairs in the nation, it would never be a success without the help of hundreds of student volunteers. Saying that, we need your help! Volunteers are able to connect with recruiters before, during, and after the fair, often without standing in lines for booths. If you have community service or society point requirements to fulfill, volunteering at the career fair is a great way to do so! Also, over $500 in gift cards will be raffled off to thank student volunteers!
