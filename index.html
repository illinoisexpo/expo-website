From our roots

Engineering Employment EXPO at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign
is one of the largest student-run career fairs in the nation.
Starting with only 30 companies in 1989, we are proud to say we now have over 400 coming this fall!

What are employers saying?

“The amount and quality of students were great! Also, everyone that worked there was SO friendly and helpful. I know how hard it is to run events like that so amazing job!”
“We met tons of high quality candidates!”
